Bio

I am currently a PhD student at UCL, supervised by Prof. Jing-Hao Xue. Before that, I received my master degree at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Wenming Yang. Previously, I obtained my B.E degree from Xidian University in 2019. I did an intership at DJI with Liliang Zhang.

My research interests include Image Manipulation, Generative Models (GAN and diffusion model), and Video Super-Resolution and Enhancement. Recently, I focus on the application of text-to-image diffusion model.
