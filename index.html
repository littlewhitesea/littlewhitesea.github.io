Bio

I am currently a PhD student at UCL, supervised by Prof. Jing-Hao Xue. Before that, I received my master degree at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Wenming Yang. Previously, I obtained my B.E degree from Xidian University in 2019. I did an internship at DJI with Liliang Zhang.

My research interests include Image Generation and Manipulation, Generative Models, and Video Super-Resolution and Enhancement. Recently, I focus on the application of text-to-image diffusion model. I am open to collaboration on these related topics. If you share similar interests and seek collaboration in these areas, please feel free to contact me.

I am currently on the job market and welcome opportunities that align with my expertise. Please feel free to reach out if you know of suitable positions.
